# 💼 NYX Commercial & Enterprise Licensing

**NYX** is proudly open-source and fiercely protected under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0)**. 

Our mission is to democratize military-grade, Zero-Knowledge communications. However, we understand that certain enterprise environments, proprietary SaaS platforms, and closed-source integrations require different legal frameworks.

## 🛑 The AGPL-3.0 Constraint (For Corporations)
Under the AGPL-3.0 license, if you modify NYX and allow users to interact with it over a network (e.g., hosting it as a SaaS or backend service), **you are legally required to release the entire source code of your service to your users.**

If your business model relies on keeping your backend or application logic closed-source (proprietary), **you cannot use the free AGPL-3.0 license.**

## 🔓 The Solution: Commercial Licensing (Dual-License)
We offer a flexible **Commercial License** that completely bypasses the AGPL-3.0 restrictions. Purchasing this license grants your organization the right to:

1. Integrate NYX into your proprietary, closed-source SaaS applications.
2. Modify, rebrand, and distribute NYX without the obligation to open-source your modifications.
3. Keep your intellectual property completely private.
4. Receive priority technical support and deployment assistance directly from the core maintainer.
